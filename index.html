GAK OVERSEAS CO LTD

For more than a decade, GA-K Overseas Co Ltd has has help producers supply paper, paperboard and pulp products to customers throughout the world.

With dedicated suppliers based in North America, Thailand, China, Korea and Indonesia, we are able to leverage our buying power and our expertise in shipping, finance and logistics to consistently procure the best deals for our clients.

Our major markets are Latin America, the Middle and Far East, and Western Europe. We value our long-term relationships with both suppliers and customers, and it is this commitment to ongoing collaboration and mutual respect that has allowed us to achieve optimal results and maximize value for all parties.

About Us

Since our founding in 2003, GA-K Overseas Co Ltd has continued to grow from strength to strength, becoming a truly global pulp, paper and board trading company. We have accomplished this by developing strategic long-term relationships with producers and mills throughout the world, and by using the latest technology to source products and improve efficiency for the benefit of our clients.
